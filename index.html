About Me

My name is Austin L Kettner.

I am an Akron, Ohio based entrepreneur who loves to meticulously crafted digital experiences. Born in Beverly Hills, CA and moving to Akron early on, I call Akron my home. I left Ohio State University within my first year to found @Quixby which was the start of my entrepreneur livelihood. From the very beginning, I have worked with two amazing business partners and friends James Hilton (@cr0wmium) and Jack Hilton (@jacklhilton).

Self taught developer, passionate UI/UX designer and general perfectionist; I best classify myself as a Product Manager.
